Today's Topic : Favourite Website

Welcome to the new episodes of my progress!

I would like to talk about some websites that I adore so much because of it's features.In today's hyperconnected world, your website is often the first encounter people have with your brand. It's your digital storefront, your storytelling canvas, and the gateway to your products, services, or ideas. Whether you're a budding entrepreneur, an established business, or an individual with a message to share, your website isn't just a virtual address—it's a reflection of your identity and the cornerstone of your online presence.

Without any further do, let's check below!

  • Notion

    • Notion is an all-in-one workspace software that combines note-taking, project management, knowledge base, and collaboration features in a single platform, offering versatility for personal and professional use. Notion is considered easier to use than some other note-taking and productivity tools because of its intuitive and flexible interface that allows users to create and customize pages, databases, and content blocks with ease, all within a single platform. Using Notion for aesthetic notes involves creating visually pleasing and customized digital notes by combining stylish fonts, colors, images, icons, and creative layouts within the Notion workspace.

  • Google Calendar

    • Google Calendar is a widely used digital calendar application that serves as a powerful scheduling tool for individuals and organizations. What sets it apart and makes it the preferred choice for many is its seamless integration with other Google services like Gmail, making it effortlessly accessible for managing appointments, events, and reminders. The calendar's standout features include the ability to share calendars and events with collaborators, set up event notifications, and even use natural language input to create events swiftly. Additionally, Google Calendar's smart scheduling capabilities help find suitable meeting times by analyzing participants' availability, streamlining the process of planning and organizing events efficiently. These features make Google Calendar a user-friendly and indispensable tool for staying organized and on top of your schedule.

  • Pierre-Louis Labonne

    • Creative freelancer Pierre-Louis Labonne immediately pulls you into his website with a big button encouraging you to click. Pierre-Louis’s site demonstrates several different web design trends for 2023 — a loading screen, nostalgia-inspired design, a customizable viewing experience, and plenty of “just for fun” interactions. Once you enter the site, you can customize the cartoon character’s face and click through different locked areas of the screen to see Pierre-Louis’s work. As you explore, you’ll discover playful interactions, and clever animations, including links to Pierre-Louis’s Twitter, Linkedin, and Webflow profiles. This 2022 Webflow Awards Technical Achievement finalist is one of the best websites, showcasing clever interactions and graphic design
