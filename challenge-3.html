Surabaya Submarine Monument

The Surabaya Submarine Monument is located in the vibrant city of Surabaya, Indonesia, offering a captivating glimpse into the country's maritime heritage. Here is some reasons why you must visit this place :

  • Naval History

    • Explore a decommissioned submarine and learn about Indonesia's naval history.

  • Unique Experience

    • Experience an educational and unique attraction, unlike traditional museums.

  • Scenic Location

Hero's Monument

The Surabaya Hero Monument stands proudly in the heart of Surabaya, Indonesia, commemorating the valiant heroes and their indomitable spirit during the Battle of Surabaya in 1945. Here is the reasons why you must visit Hero's Monument :

  • Historical Significance

    • Pay tribute to Indonesia's struggle for independence at this iconic monument.

  • Museum

    • Explore the museum within the monument complex, showcasing artifacts and history related to the nation's heroes.

  • Panoramic View

    • Enjoy panoramic views of Surabaya from the top of the monument, providing a unique perspective of the city.

Jalesveva Jayamahe Monument

The Surabaya Jalesveva Jayamahe Monument is prominently situated in Surabaya, Indonesia, celebrating the city's rich maritime tradition and the Indonesian Navy's dedication to safeguarding the nation's waters. Here is the reason why you must visit it :

  • Naval Heritage

    • Learn about Indonesia's naval heritage and achievements through the monument's displays.

  • Scenic Overlook

    • Enjoy panoramic views of Surabaya and its surroundings from this elevated vantage point.

  • Architectural Beauty

    • Appreciate the striking architectural design of the monument, making it a noteworthy landmark in the city.
